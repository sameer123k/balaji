Ghanta Ghar: Iconic Clock Tower of Jodhpur

Ghanta Ghar

Ghanta Ghar, also known as the Clock Tower, stands as a timeless symbol of Jodhpur's vibrant heritage and bustling energy. Located in the heart of the bustling Sadar Market, this majestic structure serves as a focal point for locals and visitors alike, drawing them into the colorful tapestry of Rajasthan's cultural hub

Whether you're exploring the vibrant bazaars or simply marveling at the architectural marvels, Ghanta Ghar offers a glimpse into the timeless allure of Jodhpur, where past and present seamlessly intertwine

Umaid Bhawan Palace: Opulent Oasis of Jodhpur

Umaid Bhawan Palace

Umaid Bhawan Palace stands as an epitome of grandeur and elegance amidst the arid landscapes of Jodhpur. This magnificent edifice, set against the backdrop of the majestic Mehrangarh Fort, is a testament to Rajasthan's rich architectural heritage and regal legacy. Built in the 20th century, the palace boasts a blend of Indo-Saracenic

Today, Umaid Bhawan Palace serves as a luxury heritage hotel, offering guests a glimpse into the extravagant lifestyle of the erstwhile Maharajas. From its lavish interiors to its impeccable hospitality, the palace exudes an aura of refinement and luxury, inviting visitors to immerse themselves in the grandeur of Rajasthan's royal heritage
