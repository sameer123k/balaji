Your journey to Agra begins as you depart from Jaipur with Balaji Tour & Taxi Service. Our comfortable vehicles and experienced drivers ensure a smooth and enjoyable ride. As we traverse through the picturesque landscapes of Rajasthan and Uttar Pradesh, our knowledgeable guides provide insights into the history and culture of the region.

Rise early to witness the breathtaking beauty of the Taj Mahal at sunrise, one of the most iconic landmarks in the world. Explore the pristine marble mausoleum, built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal, and marvel at its intricate craftsmanship and symmetrical design.

After breakfast, embark on a guided tour of the majestic Agra Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site that served as the main residence of the Mughal emperors. Explore the fort's impressive architecture, including the Diwan-i-Am (Hall of Public Audience) and Diwan-i-Khas (Hall of Private Audience), adorned with intricate carvings and beautiful marble work. Discover other local attractions in Agra, such as the picturesque Mehtab Bagh gardens or the serene tomb of Itmad-ud-Daulah, known as the "Baby Taj.
